Species: Human (Female)
Age: Five.
Preference: *looks at you cutely*.
Family: None known.
Status: Lost and alone.

Bio: It happened one night, as the town was sleeping. All was well with the world, until HE showed up. The mysterious person called down ancient magic upon the town, and summoned powerful beings that razed the houses and burned the crops. Phoebe was driven from her house by her parents, who died in the attack, and was about to be ripped asunder by a fearsome beast. At the last possible moment, a dragon appeared from nowhere and drove the beast away, then carried the girl off to a safe place. She was no older than three. The dragon cared for her in place of her parents, and taught her the basics of language. Upon her fifth birthday, it wiped all traces of it's existence from her mind and vanished, leaving her to wander about by herself. She has no family, no friends, and no past.

Powers: None controllable as of yet. If she gets severely emotional, she can sometimes call forth energy from her hands, in the form of glowing pulses. These fire randomly and are very dangerous.
Possible Story Ideas
Lost Little Girl: (Default) You're an explorer/adventurer/etc. traveling through a dense forest, and wander upon this poor little girl. She has no idea how she got here, and you are filled with compassion for her, to the point that you take her along with you on your journey.
(Smut Version) The above scenario pans out, but you're plagued with the urge to relieve sexual tension, and what better partner than your new friend?

The Experiment: A slightly darker story, the girl was the last in a line of experiments by a corrupt government/etc. that stripped young children from their families, doing all sorts of tests to them. The experiments were shut down, but she was left behind in a holding room somehow. You find her while combing the facility for technology.
