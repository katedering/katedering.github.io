Bio/Background

Toshiro Inoue



Toshiro Inoue (no middle name given) is a third-year student at Bayshore University, a prestigious private university located in southern Florida. He is a transfer student from Japan, having decided to set out on his own to the land of freedom, for some unknown reason...

He spends most of his time in the local public library, where he can be spotted reading various manga or fantasy novels, and where he also works part-time after school. He doesn't live on campus, instead having a modest apartment a few blocks down from where he works.

A chance meeting with a sweet girl from the nearby bakery has spiraled into a beautifully budding romance, and he is now the lucky boyfriend of one Ayumi Amberwater.

Life went on with Toshi and Ayumi, and he managed to get her to agree to move in with him. They spent a few nights of passion together, and she got him into one of her online games - which led to them meeting up with some of her game friends at a café in town. That, in turn, led to a simple dare which spiraled out of control, ending up with Toshiro sleeping with not only Ayumi, but Sarah and even her little sister Tara... and now all three are pregnant. What will become of poor Toshi?
