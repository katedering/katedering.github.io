Mika is a halfbreed between human and kitkin - similar to Neko but lacking many traits, mainly their transformation powers. However, she has some magic power; fire, ice, and electrical attacks are her strong points.

"Princess" of the kingdom of Altraan, located on the southern continent of Lanturo in the world of Finterra, Mika was raised as a soldier from age 5, she lived a life of constant fear - her mother, mad with power, had started a war of conquest, trying to claim the entire land as her own.

Mika had to fend for herself, though her magic helped her out in that regard. Shortly before her 15th birthday, she managed to sneak away from the castle and set off into the woods, hoping to escape the war.

However, fate had other plans for her...

Mika was found by none other than the Milletian Hero, Trip, and for a while he cared for her as she recovered. Though, after she had asked about her surroundings, she came to realize she was no longer in Finterra... she had been brought to the realm of Arpecee somehow, and Trip had to explain to her that she might never return home.
