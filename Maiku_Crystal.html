A Curious Little Thing...

Not much can be said about this girl, other than she is full of curiosity and wonder. Her past is unknown even to her, and despite not having much knowledge about the modern world, she is a fiercely loyal friend to those that can get close to her. She lives alone, never staying in one spot for long. Will your home be hers one day?
