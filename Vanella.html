The Shy One

Plain. Normal. Boring.
All words you would expect to hear people call Vanessa behind her back. But she doesn't care. She's perfectly happy being normal ol' her. She even gets a laugh on occasion, when her BFF Sarah calls her "Vanella" for how plain she can be sometimes.

In the rare instance she's not at home, lost in some MMO, she's curled up in a corner booth at the local coffee shop, sipping a hot cocoa and reading a book. It's times like this that could serve as the turning point in her life, if only the right person would walk up and say "hi."
Will you be the one?

Through her continued daily interactions, it has become apparent that she has a weakness for anything soft and cuddly, especially Nekomimi...

Events have happened in Vanella's story, but due to traumatic stress and heartbreak, she seems to have forgotten everything about her life up to this point, only knowing that she has a lovely little girl named Saffron.

Vanessa met up with a new friend, and shared some time with him in the park. Saffron took a liking to him, so Vanessa agreed to spend more time together.

Spending more time together has now blossomed into a beautiful relationship with Volt and his roommate Luna, and the four of them are now living together.
