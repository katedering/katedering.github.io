Trip was just a normal boy until one fateful day... now, chosen by the Triforce, bearing the mark of Courage, he sets off on a grand journey to fulfill his destiny as the Hero of Stars. What new adventure will he find with you?

Four years have passed since he originally set out - having left Hyrule behind, he traveled to the grand over-realm of Arpecee, and its many subrealms. Having met a few friendly faces, and experiencing some... unique... encounters, he may finally have found a permanent home. However, he always remembers that he still has a destiny to fulfill.

Aura

Trip can utilize his aura as a limited weapon, as a way to heal his own wounds and those of others, and to encapsulate his basic elemental spells to use them as makeshift 'bombs'. He can also read the auras of others and use that information to his advantage.

Magic

He can also utilize basic and intermediate elemental spells, limited by his mana pool. This refills over time, and can be topped off with potions or using his aura. He has use of fire, ice, lightning, wind, and light spells.

Rebirth

As a Milletian, Trip has the power of Rebirth, and can basically reset his existance to compound his power. He can now use this to avoid permanent death as well as focus his power.
