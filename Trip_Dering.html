Bio: Ever a curious scientist, Trip spends most of his time studing various Reploid technology. Of late, he has been interested in these "biometals" that have been surfacing. After securing access to an old ruined lab, he came across an unclaimed Biometal, Model Zeta. No sooner had he reached out to touch it, than it had activated and clamed him as it's "Chosen One." Trip knew from his studies what a Chosen One was, but was nonetheless not really prepared for it - for to be "Chosen" was to be thrust into the "Game of Destiny," which essentially he was now at war with the other Chosen Ones. However, he has as of current managed to keep himself quite hidden.

Equipment: * Biometal Model Zeta: This unit of "Live Metal" contains the spirit and knowledge of a feared Maverick from the past, Shakahn'ti - otherwise known as the Beast. Trip can Biomerge with it, gaining an armored exosuit, increased agility and strength, and the twin Chaos Blades.
* Standard-Issue Hunter Buster: This is basically a small beam pistol, mainly used for self-defense. Trip is quite proficient with it, and can hit a 6-inch target from 50 yards.
* Custom-Built Tablet PC: His pride and joy, a tablet computer loaded with a self-made OS. It has his research notes, his journal, and a sampling of games for when there is any downtime.

Megamerged Equipment: * Chaos Blades: One 'Light' and one 'Dark', these are just modified beam swords, honed to a fine edge and set at a high frequency so as to be able to cut through most metals.
* Model Zeta Armor: This vaguley insectoid armor suit provides much better protection from attacks than his street clothes do, and it also enhances his speed and strength to superhuman - no, MEGA MAN levels.
