Kate is a mysterious girl, having lived in seclusion most of her life. As a Milletian, she is capable of rebirth and can be a rather reckless adventurer at times. But recently, she's started to calm down and take up more 'normal' ventures. Currently she's been hired by her brother to work in his Warp Nexus tavern while he's away.

She's fairly friendly, and easy to get along with... as long as you don't make her upset.

Combat

Kate is fairly proficient with a dagger, and always has one hidden away somewhere.

Dimensional Awareness

She has the same ability as Trip in this case, and can access a pocket dimension for item storage.

Rebirth

As a Milletian, Kate has the power of Rebirth. Unlike Trip, though, she can use it for more than just avoiding mortal danger. She has been known to use it to change her appearance and even age at times.
